About the Site

Welcome to Weather Site, a fullstack website made for searching up weather by city.

This website allows you to quickly check the weather for any city around the world. With a clean, modern interface powered by the OpenWeatherMap API, you can get up-to-date weather information in seconds.

Features:

  • Real-time weather data for any city
  • Toggle between Celsius and Fahrenheit instantly
  • Keep track and control your recent searches with persistent cookies
  • Autocomplete suggestions for city names

This site is made with Flask and Python, and it is a small project I made to refresh myself on HTML elements and general flask structures.
